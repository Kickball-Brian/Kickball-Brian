A Targeted Approach to Quality Reporting

Part of our single-image cloud solution, the Reporting & Intelligence module provides an infrastructure to ensure meaningful insight into key performance indicators (KPI), driving reliability and quality across radiology ecosystems.

 

KPI Reports

This module features prefabricated reports for commonly requested KPI review such as the following:

  • Turnaround time
  • Volume by physician referral
  • Technologist quality
  • Peer review
 

Customizable Reports

We recognize that some facilities’ quality efforts will require a different focus. That’s where our fully customizable reports come in. Upon request, our service technicians work with you and your quality team to develop reports customized to your facility’s or system’s needs, delivering a more targeted and deliberate review process.

 

No Downtime Implementation

As with all of BRIT’s offerings, the VNA module is easily integrated with your existing infrastructure, including external imaging systems, meaning little to no downtime for implementation.
